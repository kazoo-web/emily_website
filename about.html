About

This is where everything happens.

There's a question I get sometimes: "Why do you care so much?"

The answer is simple. Because this is where everything happens.

Every company, every team, every moment of growth or failure — at its core, it comes down to people. Their patterns, their relationships, their ability to navigate pressure, conflict, and change. And most people are operating without visibility into the very things shaping their outcomes.

That's where I come in. I spent over a decade as a therapist, working deeply with individuals and couples. That training gave me a way of seeing patterns, dynamics, and underlying structures that are often invisible but highly impactful.

Over time, I became increasingly drawn to high-performance, high-pressure environments — startups, where the stakes are higher, the speed is faster, and the interpersonal dynamics are amplified.

What I do now is sit at the intersection of depth and performance, insight and execution. This is not passive work. It requires openness, honesty, and a willingness to experience discomfort.

But when it works, it changes everything. Decisions become clearer, relationships stabilize, leadership strengthens. Teams function at a higher level, and you find yourself leading with more confidence and, often, more joy.

I care deeply about this work because I've seen what happens when people avoid it — and what becomes possible when they don't. If I can help someone see even one pattern that's been holding them back, that's meaningful. And if we can shift it, that's where real change begins.

Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist. Spent over a decade in clinical practice working with individuals and couples navigating complex dynamics, high-stakes decisions, and lasting change.

Now working with founders, co-founders, and leadership teams — bringing that same depth into high-performance environments where the work compounds.
