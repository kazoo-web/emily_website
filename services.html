Services

1:1 Founder Coaching

High-trust, high-impact support for founders navigating pressure, decision-making, identity, and leadership. We work on what's actually in the way, starting with building insight, ending with lasting change, and maintaining with continued support.

This is a space to think clearly in environments where clarity is rare.

We work on the real constraints: decision paralysis, identity tension, leadership gaps, difficult relationships, and the internal pressure that comes with building something meaningful.

My role is not just to support you—but to help you see what you cannot see. We'll identify patterns, challenge assumptions, and build the internal and interpersonal skills required to lead effectively as your company grows.

This work is tailored, direct, and often confronting—in the best way.

Co-Founder Dynamics

For teams experiencing friction, misalignment, or breakdown. We surface what's unspoken and rebuild a more functional, durable partnership, focusing on the interpersonal dynamics getting in the way of relating effectively.

Co-founder relationships are one of the highest-leverage, and highest-risk, variables in a company.

When things start to break down, it's rarely about the surface-level issue. It's about what's underneath: unspoken expectations, resentment, misaligned values, or communication patterns that no longer work.

I help co-founders have the conversations they've been avoiding, in a way that's structured, honest, and productive.

The goal is to help transform the co-founding relationship into something more functional: a resilient partnership that can actually sustain the company.

Team & Leadership Support

Coaching across your organization to strengthen communication, reduce friction, and support healthier, more effective team dynamics.

As companies scale, interpersonal complexity increases. What worked at 5 people breaks at 15. What worked at 15 breaks at 50.

I work across teams to reduce friction, strengthen communication, and support leaders in managing the human side of growth.

This can include:

  • Coaching available to team members
  • Facilitating difficult or stuck conversations
  • Supporting leadership alignment
  • Addressing high-conflict dynamics

The focus is simple: build a team that actually works.
