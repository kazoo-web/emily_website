About Approach Connect

Services & Approach

Moving through friction,
not around it

I work with founders and teams in high-growth, high-pressure environments — where what's most in the way is rarely visible. My approach blends therapeutic depth with direct, high-leverage coaching. I see patterns others miss, name what others avoid, and help founders move through friction instead of around it. I'm often brought in when teams are stuck: highly capable leaders who care deeply but can't seem to get on the same page. This is where the real work happens.

1:1 Founder Coaching

High-trust, high-impact support for founders navigating pressure, decision-making, identity, and leadership. We work on what's actually in the way — decision paralysis, identity tension, leadership gaps, difficult relationships, and the internal pressure that comes with building something meaningful. My role is not just to support you but to help you see what you cannot see, challenge assumptions, and build the skills required to lead effectively as your company grows.

Co-Founder Dynamics

For teams experiencing friction, misalignment, or breakdown. Co-founder relationships are one of the highest-leverage and highest-risk variables in a company — when things start to break down, it's rarely about the surface-level issue. I help co-founders have the conversations they've been avoiding, in a way that's structured, honest, and productive, and work toward a partnership that's more functional and more durable.

Team & Leadership Support

As companies scale, interpersonal complexity increases. What worked at 5 people breaks at 15. I work across teams to reduce friction, strengthen communication, and support leaders in managing the human side of growth — through facilitated conversations, leadership coaching, and direct work on the dynamics that determine how a team actually functions together.
