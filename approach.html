Approach

The heart of my coaching

My work blends therapeutic depth with direct, high-leverage coaching. With a background in Marriage and Family Therapy, I bring a way of seeing patterns, dynamics, and underlying tensions that are difficult to see and even more difficult to speak to. I surface what's happening beneath the surface, name what others avoid, and help you move through friction instead of around it.

My approach

This work is adaptive and multifaceted. I work 1:1 with founders, with co-founders navigating complexity or conflict, and with leadership teams as companies scale. The focus is not just on individual growth, but on how people function together because that's what ultimately determines outcomes. I step into high-pressure environments to help teams think more clearly, communicate more effectively, and operate productively.

Working together

This is not surface-level coaching. It's a space to slow down, see clearly, and elucidate what's actually in the way. The work is direct, honest, and at times uncomfortable. But it can also be fun, joyous and playful, leading to stronger leadership, more stable relationships, and a more sustainable way of being.

If you're willing to look at what's really going on, we'll be able to do meaningful work together.
